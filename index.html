Privacy

Last updated February 2026

Just One Thing is built with your privacy as a foundation, not an afterthought.

Your Data

Everything you create — your daily tasks and thoughts — lives on your device. If you have iCloud enabled, it syncs privately via Apple's CloudKit. We never see it.

What We Don't Do

  • Collect analytics or usage data
  • Use third-party tracking or advertising
  • Sell, share, or transfer your data
  • Access your content — ever

iCloud Sync

iCloud sync is managed entirely by Apple under Apple's Privacy Policy. You can disable it any time in device settings.
