Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Just One Thing is designed with your privacy in mind. Here's what you need to know:

Data We Collect

Just One Thing stores only the data you create — your daily tasks and thoughts. This data lives on your device and, if you have iCloud enabled, syncs privately via Apple's CloudKit.

What We Don't Do

  • We do not collect analytics or usage data
  • We do not use third-party tracking or advertising SDKs
  • We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties
  • We do not have access to your data — it stays on your device and in your private iCloud account

iCloud Sync

If you use iCloud, your data syncs across your devices using Apple's CloudKit infrastructure. This sync is managed entirely by Apple and subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. You can disable iCloud sync at any time in your device settings.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@johannystrom.se.
